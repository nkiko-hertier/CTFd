CTFd is the most interactive platform for cyber challenges, designed to help you build.

CTFd is a Capture The Flag (CTF) framework that provides a platform for hosting cybersecurity competitions. It offers a user-friendly interface for participants to solve challenges, track their progress, and compete against others. CTFd is widely used in the cybersecurity community for educational purposes, skill development, and team-building exercises.

Why CTFd?

A powerful platform for building and managing cybersecurity competitions, with features designed for both beginners and experts.

Skill-based Challenges

A wide range of challenges from binary exploitation and web hacking to forensics and cryptography.

Team Collaboration

Compete solo or create teams to collaborate, strategize, and solve challenges together.

Dynamic Scoreboard

Track your progress and rankings in real-time on our dynamic and interactive scoreboard.
